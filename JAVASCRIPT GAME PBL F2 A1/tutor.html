Shark Feast: Complete Game Guide

About Shark Feast

Shark Feast is an underwater survival game where you start as a small shark pup and evolve into the ultimate apex predator. Navigate through three distinct ocean zones, each with unique challenges and rewards.

3 Ocean Zones

Progress from shallow reefs to the mysterious deep sea

15+ Fish Types

Each with unique behaviors and point values

Special Abilities

Unlock boosts, weapons, and evolutions

Advanced Controls

  • Arrow Keys / WASD: Move the shark in all directions
  • Spacebar: Boost (if available)
  • Double Tap Arrow Keys: Quick dash in that direction (consumes boost)
  • E Key: Fire weapon when equipped (deep zone only)
  • Shift + Direction: Precision movement (slower but more controlled)
  • Mouse Click: Interact with menus/buttons
  • ESC Key: Pause the game
Warning: Boosting near obstacles may cause collision damage!

Advanced Gameplay

Hunger System

  • Start as a small shark in the shallow waters
  • Eat smaller fish to grow your shark and gain points
  • Avoid larger predators that can eat you
  • Hunger depletes faster in deeper zones
  • Eating while full gives bonus points
  • Starving slows your movement speed
Pro Tip: Chain eating multiple fish quickly for combo bonuses!

Special Items

  • Golden Fish: 5x point value + temporary invincibility
  • Jellyfish: Stuns nearby predators (handle with care!)
  • Treasure Chest: Random power-up or bonus points

Advanced Strategies

Zone-Specific Tactics

  • Shallow Reef: Use coral formations to trap schools of fish
  • Mid Ocean: Bait predators into chasing you toward weapon drops
  • Deep Sea: Conserve ammo - only shoot when the boss is vulnerable

Score Multipliers

  • 5+ fish combo: 1.5x multiplier
  • No damage taken: 2x zone completion bonus
  • All golden fish eaten: 3x final score

Technical Information

  • Save System: Progress autosaves between zones
  • Performance: Disable shadows in settings for better FPS
  • Controls: Supports gamepads (XInput/DirectInput)

Tips & Tricks

  • Watch your hunger meter - don't let it empty completely
  • Different fish give different point values
  • Use boost strategically to escape danger or catch prey
  • Some power-ups appear randomly - grab them when you can
  • Bigger sharks can break certain obstacles

Progression System

  • Earn XP for everything you eat
  • Level up to unlock new shark species
  • Each species has unique abilities and stats
  • Complete achievements for special rewards
  • Compete with friends on the leaderboard
