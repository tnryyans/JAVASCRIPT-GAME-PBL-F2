About The Game

Shark Feast is an immersive underwater survival game where you take control of a hungry shark navigating through increasingly dangerous ocean depths. Starting as a small reef shark, you must hunt to survive, grow larger, and eventually dominate the entire ocean ecosystem.

With intuitive controls and progressively challenging gameplay, Shark Feast offers:

  • Dynamic Growth System: Watch your shark evolve in real-time as you consume prey
  • Three Distinct Zones: From sunlit coral reefs to the pitch-black abyss
  • Epic Boss Battles: Test your skills against massive deep-sea creatures
  • Survival Mechanics: Manage your health and energy while hunting

Game Development Trivia

The ocean zones in Shark Feast were inspired by real marine biology classifications. Developers consulted with marine biologists to create authentic underwater ecosystems.

Explore The Ocean Depths

Sunlit Zone (0-200m)

The vibrant coral reefs where your journey begins. Hunt tropical fish in crystal clear waters, but beware of fishing nets and human divers!

Twilight Zone (200-1000m)

Darker waters with mysterious creatures. Here you'll encounter larger prey and your first real predators. Use your boost ability strategically!

Midnight Zone (1000m+)

The deepest, most dangerous part of the ocean. Only the largest sharks can survive here, where bioluminescent creatures and massive bosses await.

Shark Evolution Path

Your shark grows through 5 distinct size classes, each with unique abilities:

  • Small Shark (1-2m): Fast but fragile - stick to small fish
  • Medium Shark (2-4m): Balanced stats - can take on jellyfish
  • Large Shark (4-6m): Strong bite - hunts turtles and seals
  • Giant Shark (6-8m): Massive strength - attacks small boats
  • Megalodon (8m+): Ultimate predator - dominates all ocean life

Shark Fact

The prehistoric Megalodon could grow up to 18 meters (60 feet) long - twice the size of your in-game Megalodon! Maybe we'll add an even larger prehistoric mode in a future update.
